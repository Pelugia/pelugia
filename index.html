Questions

Everything you need
to know.

What size of leadership team is ideal for a Pelugia retreat?
Our retreats are designed for intimate leadership groups — typically 4 to 14 executives. This size ensures genuine connection and meaningful conversation without the dynamic of a large corporate event. If your group is larger, we can discuss bespoke options.
How far in advance should we book?
We recommend reaching out at least 8–12 weeks ahead of your preferred dates to ensure availability at our preferred venues, particularly for peak season on the Costa del Sol (May–October). For larger or more complex retreats, 4–6 months is ideal.
Can you accommodate dietary requirements and accessibility needs?
Absolutely. We collect detailed requirements from every participant during the planning phase and coordinate directly with chefs, venues, and transport providers to ensure every detail is taken care of — including mobility needs, dietary restrictions, and personal preferences.
Is there a facilitation or coaching component to the retreat?
Our standard retreats are designed for organic, unstructured connection — we believe the environment does the work. If you'd like a structured leadership workshop or an external executive coach, we can integrate this into the programme at an additional investment.
Are the pricing tiers fixed, or is everything bespoke?
The pricing shown reflects starting points for each format. Every retreat is priced individually based on your group size, venue, dates, and specific inclusions. The discovery call allows us to build an accurate, transparent proposal for your exact requirements.
Where exactly do your retreats take place?
All Pelugia retreats are based in and around Marbella, on Spain's Costa del Sol. Our three core settings are the Golden Mile (private villas and beach clubs), Ronda and the Serranía de Ronda (wine estates and dramatic landscapes), and Puerto Banús (private yacht charters). Each offers a completely different energy — we'll guide you to the right fit for your team.
